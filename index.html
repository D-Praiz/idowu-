About

I’m a passionate Front-End Developer with a keen eye for design, user experience, and data-driven insights. With a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern libraries like React, I specialize in building intuitive, responsive, and scalable web applications.

In addition to front-end development, I’m also skilled in data science and programming, enabling me to analyze, interpret, and leverage data to inform design decisions and improve product performance. My experience with Python, SQL, and data visualization tools complements my development work, making me a well-rounded tech professional.

Over the years, I’ve developed and maintained visually engaging, accessible, and high-performing websites for businesses across different industries. I focus on writing clean, reusable code and turning UI/UX designs into pixel-perfect reality.

Whether it’s breathing life into static designs, collaborating with back-end teams, optimizing performance across devices, or drawing insights from user data, I bring energy and excellence to every line of code. I’m a strong believer in continuous learning, and I stay up to date with industry trends and best practices.
